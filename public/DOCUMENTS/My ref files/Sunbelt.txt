To get around this please do the following. 

1. Click start and then click run. 
2. In the field provided, please type Services.msc and then hit enter.
3. Please look for Sunbelt Personal Firewall and double click on it.
4. Click on the recovery tab. Change each of the fields first failure, second failure, and third failure from take no action to run a program. 
5. Under program type c:\windows\system32\net.exe start SPF4.
6. Click ok on the bottom of that window to apply the settings. 
7. Click on the X in the upper right hand corner of the services window to close it. 
8. Please restart your computer.
