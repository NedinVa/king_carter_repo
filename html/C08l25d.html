Robert Carter to Francis Lee, September 25, 1708

Rappa [hannock, Lancaster County, Virginia]

September 25, 1708

Francis Lee

     My last of 25 of May went by the Carter , brot as I hope bill of Ladin [g for 18]
hogsheads of Tobbacco since I have Yors: by the Fleet wth. the Copys I desired f [or which]
my thanks. The Young Wormeleys have their Estate under their own [direction]
suppose by the directions of their mother, & no doubt You are among thei [r particular?]
Friends, tis time for them to be acquainted with business & I'm clear out [This will]
be the last letter on their Accot.I remain


Yor Affect [iona]t [e]. Coun [tryman &]
humble Servt.
