Robert Carter to Thomas Corbin, September 25, 1708

[Rappahannock, Lancaster County, Virginia]
[September 25, 1708]
Thomas Corbin

     I have done Concerning my Self with Young Wormeleys affairs, their Estate is e [ntire]
ly under their own Govermt. & indeed `tis time they addict themselves to business & so I take
my leave of it & You too from any further writing to You on their Accot: remaining


Yor affectiont Countryman &
humble Servant

Notes



This letter precedes another of this date in the letter book on the same theme.
