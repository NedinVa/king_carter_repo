Robert Carter to John Goodwin, September 25, 1708

[Rappahannock, Lancaster County, Virginia]
[September 25, 1708]
John Goodwin

     The Young Wormeleys are now Supravisors of their own Estate I ha [ve told]
them nor will You further hear from me on their Accot only have now [to tell]
You I have drawn on You for 4£ 10p to Doctor [John] Lomax being a debt due from [the estate]
& You are to pay it accordingly Charging it to the Debit thereof


I am
Yor humble Servant

Notes



This letter precedes another of this date in the letter book on the same theme.
