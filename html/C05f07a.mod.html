William Robertson to Robert Carter and John Lightfoot

Williamsburgh [, Virginia] June the 27th 1705

Sr.

His Excellency haveing just now received advice from Capt. Josiah Moon of Her Majestys Ship the Oxford that he and another man of war arrived last night at
Place: Kiquotan finds it necessary for Her Majestys Service to have a meeting of the Councill here on Saturday the 30th Instant at which he expects Your Honors will not fail to give Your attendance which by His Excellencys Command is all from
Your Honrs. most obedient humble Servant
WIL ROBERTSON
